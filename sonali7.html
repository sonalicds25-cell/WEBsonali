Dr.A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India | Former President of India

About

Dr.A.P.J.Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a leading role in India's missile and nuclear weapon programs. He was widely know as the "Missile Man of India".
